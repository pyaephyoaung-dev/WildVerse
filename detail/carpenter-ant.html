Carpenter Ant

Camponotus spp.

Carpenter ants are large ants known for their ability to excavate wood to form nests. Unlike termites, they do not consume wood but remove it to create galleries.

Scientific NameCamponotus spp.
Class / OrderInsecta, Hymenoptera
HabitatForests and urban wooden structures
DietOmnivore — plant and insect material
Weight3-10 mg
Size6–12 mm length
SpeedUp to 3 km/h
Lifespan7-10 years (queen)
RangeWorldwide, mostly temperate regions
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationAbundant
