Olm

Proteus anguinus

The Olm is a blind, cave-dwelling salamander found in European underground waters. It is adapted to life in darkness with a pale, elongated body and highly sensitive senses.

Scientific NameProteus anguinus
Class / OrderAmphibia, Urodela
HabitatSubterranean caves and streams
DietCarnivore — small aquatic invertebrates
Weight20-25 g
Size20–30 cm length
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan50-100 years
RangeDinaric Alps, Central and Southeastern Europe
Conservation StatusVU — Vulnerable
PopulationUnknown; vulnerable
