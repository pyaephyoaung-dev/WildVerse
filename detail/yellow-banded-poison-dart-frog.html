Yellow-banded Poison Dart Frog

Dendrobates leucomelas

Famous for its bright yellow and black bands, this toxic frog is native to northern South America. It uses its coloration to warn predators of its toxicity.

Scientific NameDendrobates leucomelas
Class / OrderAmphibia, Anura
HabitatTropical rainforests
DietCarnivore — ants, termites, and other small insects
Weight3-5 g
Size3–4 cm body length
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan8-12 years
RangeVenezuela, Guyana, Brazil
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown
