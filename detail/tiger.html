Bengal Tiger

Panthera tigris tigris

The Bengal tiger is the most numerous tiger subspecies, found primarily in India. Known for their distinctive orange coats with black stripes, they are skilled hunters and prefer dense forests and grasslands.

Scientific NamePanthera tigris tigris
Class / OrderMammalia, Carnivora
HabitatTropicalForests & Grasslands
DietCarnivore — large ungulates
Weight180–250 kg
Size2.7–3.1 m body length
SpeedUp to 60 km/h
Lifespan10–15 years
RangeIndia, Bangladesh, Nepal, Bhutan
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
Population~2,500 in the wild

Interesting Facts

Every tiger has unique stripe patterns.
