Western Fence Lizard

Sceloporus occidentalis

The Western Fence Lizard is a common lizard of the western United States, known for its blue belly patches. It is often found on fences and rocks in dry habitats.

Scientific NameSceloporus occidentalis
Class / OrderReptilia, Squamata
HabitatGrasslands and woodlands
DietInsectivore — insects and spiders
Weight10-20 g
Size13–17 cm total length
SpeedFast runner
Lifespan5-7 years
RangeWestern United States
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationAbundant
