Ornate Box Turtle

Terrapene ornata

The Ornate Box Turtle is a terrestrial turtle with a beautifully patterned shell, found primarily in the central United States. It is a slow-moving omnivore with a strong, hinged shell.

Scientific NameTerrapene ornata
Class / OrderReptilia, Testudines
HabitatGrasslands and prairies
DietOmnivore — plants, insects, small animals
Weight0.5-1.1 kg
Size12–15 cm carapace length
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan30-40 years
RangeCentral United States
Conservation StatusNT — Near Threatened
PopulationUnknown

Interesting Facts

It can completely close its shell for protection.
