Uromastyx

Uromastyx spp.

Uromastyx, also called spiny-tailed lizards, are herbivorous reptiles adapted to arid environments in Africa and the Middle East. They are known for their thick, spiked tails used in defense.

Scientific NameUromastyx spp.
Class / OrderReptilia, Squamata
HabitatDeserts and rocky areas
DietHerbivore — leaves, flowers, seeds
Weight0.5-2 kg
Size20–90 cm total length depending on species
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan15-20 years
RangeNorth Africa and Middle East
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown

Interesting Facts

Uromastyx are primarily herbivores.
They use their tails to block burrow entrances.
They bask extensively to regulate body temperature.
They can survive with very little water.
