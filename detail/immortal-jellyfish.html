Immortal Jellyfish

Turritopsis dohrnii

This small jellyfish species can revert its cells to an earlier stage of development, potentially allowing it to bypass death, earning it the nickname 'immortal jellyfish.'

Scientific NameTurritopsis dohrnii
Class / OrderScyphozoa, Semaeostomeae
HabitatCoastal waters worldwide
DietCarnivore — plankton and small fish
WeightFew grams
SizeUp to 4.5 mm bell diameter
SpeedSlow-moving (pulsating swimming)
LifespanPotentially indefinite
RangeWorldwide, especially temperate waters
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown

Interesting Facts

Can transform adult cells back into immature polyps.
