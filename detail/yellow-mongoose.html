Yellow Mongoose

Cynictis penicillata

The yellow mongoose is a small carnivorous mammal inhabiting open plains and semi-deserts in southern Africa. It is social and lives in colonies underground. Its diet includes insects, small rodents, and reptiles.

Interesting Facts

Yellow mongooses dig complex burrow systems.
They use alarm calls to warn of predators.
They are primarily diurnal animals.
Their yellow fur provides camouflage in dry grass.
