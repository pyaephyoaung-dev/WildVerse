Castoroides

Castoroides ohioensis

Known as the giant beaver, Castoroides was a massive rodent that lived in North America during the Pleistocene. It was much larger than modern beavers but likely had similar ecological roles.

Interesting Facts

Castoroides could reach lengths up to 2.5 meters including tail.
It lacked the flattened tail typical of modern beavers.
Diet mainly consisted of aquatic plants and tree bark.
Extinct approximately 10,000 years ago.
