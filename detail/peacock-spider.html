Peacock Spider

Maratus volans

This tiny jumping spider is famous for its colorful courtship display performed by males to attract females. It uses its abdominal flaps to create an elaborate visual show.

Scientific NameMaratus volans
Class / OrderArachnida, Araneae
HabitatGrasslands and shrublands
DietCarnivore — small insects
Weight2-5 mg
Size3–5 mm body length
SpeedFast for its size
Lifespan1 year
RangeAustralia
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown

Interesting Facts

Males display bright colors and dance during mating rituals.
