Tarsier

Tarsius spp.

Tarsiers are small primates with enormous eyes adapted for nocturnal hunting of insects and small animals. They have elongated fingers and strong hind legs for leaping between trees. Tarsiers are found in Southeast Asian forests.

Scientific NameTarsius spp.
Class / OrderMammalia, Primates

Interesting Facts

Tarsiers can rotate their heads almost 180 degrees.
Their eyes are each larger than their brain.
They communicate with ultrasonic vocalizations.
Tarsiers are solitary and territorial.
