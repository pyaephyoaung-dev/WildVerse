Madagascar Hissing Cockroach

Gromphadorhina portentosa

This large cockroach species is famous for its hissing sound produced by expelling air through spiracles. It is a decomposer living on forest floors.

Scientific NameGromphadorhina portentosa
Class / OrderInsecta, Blattodea
HabitatTropical forests
DietOmnivore — decaying plant material, fruits
Weight15-30 g
Size7–10 cm length
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan2-5 years
RangeMadagascar
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown

Interesting Facts
