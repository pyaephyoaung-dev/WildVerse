Zigzag Salamander

Plethodon dorsalis

This terrestrial salamander is named for the zigzagging light pattern on its dark back. It is commonly found in deciduous forests across the central United States.

Scientific NamePlethodon dorsalis
Class / OrderAmphibia, Caudata
HabitatDeciduous forests
DietCarnivore — small invertebrates
Weight5-15 g
Size8–12 cm body length
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan6-10 years
