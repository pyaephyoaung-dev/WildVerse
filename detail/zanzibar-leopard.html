Zanzibar Leopard

Panthera pardus adersi

The Zanzibar leopard is a subspecies of leopard once endemic to Zanzibar Island. Possibly extinct, it was last reliably seen in the 1990s and threatened by hunting and habitat loss.

Scientific NamePanthera pardus adersi
Class / OrderMammalia, Carnivora
