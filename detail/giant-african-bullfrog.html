Giant African Bullfrog

Pyxicephalus adspersus

One of the largest frogs in Africa, the Giant African Bullfrog is known for its bulky size and voracious appetite. Males guard tadpoles aggressively.

Scientific NamePyxicephalus adspersus
Class / OrderAmphibia, Anura
HabitatSavannahs and grasslands
DietCarnivore — insects, small vertebrates
Weight1.4-1.9 kg
SizeUp to 24 cm snout-vent length
SpeedModerate
Lifespan10-15 years
RangeSouthern Africa
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown

Interesting Facts

Can burrow and aestivate during dry periods.
