Italian Crested Newt

Triturus carnifex

This medium-sized newt is known for the male's prominent dorsal crest during breeding season. It inhabits ponds and slow-moving waters in Italy and surrounding regions.

Scientific NameTriturus carnifex
Class / OrderAmphibia, Caudata
HabitatPonds and freshwater bodies
DietCarnivore — aquatic invertebrates
Weight15-40 g
Size10–13 cm total length
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan8-12 years
RangeItaly, Slovenia, Croatia
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown
