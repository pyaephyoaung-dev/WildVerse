Elasmotherium

Elasmotherium sibiricum

Elasmotherium, sometimes called the 'Siberian unicorn', was a giant rhinoceros with a large single horn. It roamed the Eurasian steppes during the Late Pleistocene and likely grazed on tough grasses.

Interesting Facts

Elasmotherium's horn could have been up to 2 meters long.
It had high-crowned teeth adapted for grazing abrasive vegetation.
Extinct roughly 39,000 years ago.
It was among the last surviving members of the rhinoceros subfamily Elasmotheriinae.
