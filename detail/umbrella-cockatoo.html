Umbrella Cockatoo

Cacatua alba

The Umbrella Cockatoo is a striking white parrot notable for its large crest that can be raised like an umbrella. Native to Indonesia, it is popular in aviculture but vulnerable in the wild.

Scientific NameCacatua alba
Class / OrderAves, Psittaciformes
HabitatTropical lowland forests
DietOmnivore — seeds, nuts, fruits, insects
Weight600-900 g
Size45–50 cm body length
SpeedN/A
Lifespan40-60 years

Interesting Facts

They use their curved bill to crack hard seeds and nuts.
Umbrella Cockatoos are highly intelligent and social.
Their crest is used for communication and display.
Habitat loss and trapping threaten wild populations.
