Long-eared Jerboa

Euchoreutes naso

The long-eared jerboa is a tiny rodent with enormous ears and a long tail, specialized for desert life. It uses its large ears for thermoregulation and acute hearing to avoid predators. It is a nocturnal insectivore with remarkable jumping ability.
