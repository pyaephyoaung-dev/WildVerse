Emperor Scorpion

Pandinus imperator

One of the largest scorpion species, the Emperor Scorpion is notable for its glossy black exoskeleton and relatively mild venom. It is a nocturnal predator.

Scientific NamePandinus imperator
Class / OrderArachnida, Scorpiones
HabitatTropical rainforests and savannas
DietCarnivore — insects, spiders, small vertebrates
Weight30-60 g
Size18–20 cm length
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan6-8 years
RangeWest Africa
Conservation StatusVU — Vulnerable
PopulationUnknown
