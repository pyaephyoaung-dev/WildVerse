Harpy Eagle

Harpia harpyja

The Harpy Eagle is a powerful raptor with striking black and white plumage and massive talons. It inhabits tropical rainforests and preys on monkeys, sloths, and large birds.

Scientific NameHarpia harpyja
Class / OrderAves, Accipitriformes
HabitatTropical rainforests
DietCarnivore — medium-sized mammals and birds
Weight6-9 kg
Size86–107 cm body length, wingspan 176–224 cm
Speed80 km/h (flight)
Lifespan25-35 years
RangeCentral and South America, from Mexico to Brazil
Conservation StatusVU — Vulnerable
PopulationVulnerable, ~5,000 individuals

Interesting Facts

It is one of the largest and most powerful eagles worldwide.
Harpy Eagles have legs as thick as a human wrist.
They build enormous nests high in emergent trees.
Their populations are threatened by habitat loss and hunting.
