Yangtze River Dolphin

Lipotes vexillifer

Also known as the baiji, the Yangtze River dolphin was a freshwater dolphin native to the Yangtze River. It is considered functionally extinct due to habitat loss and pollution.

Scientific NameLipotes vexillifer
Class / OrderMammalia, Cetacea
HabitatFreshwater river
DietCarnivore — fish
Weight135-230 kg
Size2.3 m length
SpeedUp to 55 km/h
Lifespan20-30 years
RangeYangtze River, China
