Xiphosura (Horseshoe Crab)

Limulus polyphemus

Though more closely related to arachnids than crabs, horseshoe crabs are ancient marine arthropods vital for biomedical uses and coastal ecosystems.

Scientific NameLimulus polyphemus
Class / OrderMerostomata, Xiphosurida
HabitatShallow coastal waters and estuaries
DietOmnivore — worms, mollusks, and detritus
WeightUp to 5 kg
SizeUp to 60 cm length
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan20-40 years
RangeWestern Atlantic and Gulf of Mexico
Conservation StatusVU — Vulnerable
PopulationUnknown
