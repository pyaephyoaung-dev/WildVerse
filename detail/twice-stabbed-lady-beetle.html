Twice-stabbed Lady Beetle

Chilocorus stigma

This small beetle is black with two red spots resembling stab marks. It feeds on scale insects and is beneficial for controlling pests.

Scientific NameChilocorus stigma
Class / OrderInsecta, Coleoptera
HabitatForests and gardens
DietCarnivore — aphids and scale insects
Weight5-10 mg
Size4–6 mm length
SpeedModerate flyer
Lifespan1 year
RangeNorth America
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown
