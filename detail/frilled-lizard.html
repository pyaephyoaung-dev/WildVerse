Frilled Lizard

Chlamydosaurus kingii

The Frilled Lizard is famous for the large frill around its neck, which it displays when threatened. Native to northern Australia and New Guinea, it is arboreal and primarily insectivorous.

Scientific NameChlamydosaurus kingii
Class / OrderReptilia, Squamata
