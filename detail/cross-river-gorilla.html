Cross River Gorilla

Gorilla gorilla diehli

The Cross River gorilla is a rare subspecies of western gorilla found in forested regions along the Nigeria-Cameroon border. It is critically endangered with fewer than 300 individuals remaining.

Scientific NameGorilla gorilla diehli
Class / OrderMammalia, Primates
HabitatMontane and lowland forests
DietHerbivore — leaves, fruits, stems
Weight100-160 kg
Size1.2–1.8 m height
SpeedSlow-moving; can run up to 25 km/h briefly
Lifespan35-40 years
RangeNigeria and Cameroon border region
Conservation StatusCR — Critically Endangered
Population~200–300
