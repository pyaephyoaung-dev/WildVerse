African Lion

Panthera leo

The African lion is the second-largest living cat after the tiger. Highly social, lions live in groups called prides, roaming savanna and grasslands across Sub-Saharan Africa.

Scientific NamePanthera leo
Class / OrderMammalia, Carnivora
HabitatSavanna & Grassland
DietCarnivore — large ungulates
Weight120–250 kg
Size1.4–2.5 m body length
SpeedUp to 80 km/h
Lifespan10–14 years
RangeSub-Saharan Africa
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
Population~20,000 in the wild

Interesting Facts

A lion's roar can be heard from up to 8 km away.
Female lions do most of the hunting for the pride.
Lions sleep up to 20 hours per day to conserve energy.
A pride can contain up to 40 individuals.
