Purple Frog

Nasikabatrachus sahyadrensis

This unusual frog spends most of its life underground and was only discovered in 2003. It has a bloated body and a pointed snout adapted for burrowing in India’s Western Ghats.

Scientific NameNasikabatrachus sahyadrensis
Class / OrderAmphibia, Anura
HabitatSubterranean burrows in forested areas
DietCarnivore — primarily termites and ants
Weight50-150 g
Size7.5 cm body length
SpeedSlow-moving
LifespanUnknown
RangeWestern Ghats, India
Conservation StatusEN — Endangered
