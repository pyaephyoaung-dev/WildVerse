Red Fox

Vulpes vulpes

The red fox is a small to medium-sized canid with a reddish coat and a bushy tail. They are highly adaptable and found in various habitats across the Northern Hemisphere.

Scientific NameVulpes vulpes
Class / OrderMammalia, Carnivora
HabitatForests, grasslands, mountains, deserts, and urban areas
DietOmnivore — small mammals, birds, fruits
Weight3-14 kg
Size45–90 cm body length
SpeedUp to 50 km/h
Lifespan3–5 years (wild), up to 10–12 years (captivity)
RangeNorth America, Europe, Asia, North Africa, Australia
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationStable, widespread

Interesting Facts

Red foxes are excellent climbers.
They have a keen sense of hearing and smell.
Red Foxes are solitary animals except during mating season.
