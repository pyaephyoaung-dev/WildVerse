Margay

Leopardus wiedii

The margay is a small wild cat native to Central and South America, known for its arboreal lifestyle. It is an excellent climber and can descend trees headfirst. Margays primarily hunt small mammals, birds, and reptiles at night.

Interesting Facts

Margays can rotate their ankles 180 degrees.
They are one of the most arboreal of all cats.
Their vocalizations include meows and purrs.
Margays mimic the calls of their prey.
