Warty Sea Cucumber

Parastichopus parvimensis

This sea cucumber species has a rough, warty skin texture and is a benthic detritivore, playing a vital role in recycling nutrients on the ocean floor.

Scientific NameParastichopus parvimensis
Class / OrderHolothuroidea, Dendrochirotida
HabitatSandy and muddy sea bottoms
DietDetritivore — organic matter in sediment
WeightUp to 1 kg
SizeUp to 30 cm length
SpeedSlow-moving
LifespanUp to 10 years
RangeEastern Pacific Ocean
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown
