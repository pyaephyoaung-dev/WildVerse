Vietnamese Mossy Frog

Theloderma corticale

Known for its camouflaged skin resembling moss, this frog inhabits rocky streams in Vietnam. It uses its unique appearance to blend into mossy environments.

Scientific NameTheloderma corticale
Class / OrderAmphibia, Anura
HabitatRocky streams and forests
DietCarnivore — insects and arthropods
Weight30-50 g
Size6–8 cm body length
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan8-10 years
RangeNorthern Vietnam
