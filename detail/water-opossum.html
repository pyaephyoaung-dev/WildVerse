Water Opossum

Chironectes minimus

Also known as the yapok, the water opossum is a unique marsupial adapted for aquatic life. It has webbed hind feet and a waterproof pouch for carrying young. This nocturnal creature feeds on fish, crustaceans, and aquatic insects.

Interesting Facts

Water opossums are the only aquatic marsupials.
Their pouches have waterproof lining to protect young.
They hunt by diving underwater at night.
They have excellent swimming abilities.
