Binturong

Arctictis binturong

Also called the 'bearcat,' the binturong is a large arboreal mammal with a distinctive prehensile tail. It emits a unique popcorn-like scent used for marking territory. Binturongs are omnivores, feeding on fruits, small animals, and eggs.

Scientific NameArctictis binturong

HabitatTropical rainforests

Interesting Facts

Their prehensile tail helps them balance in trees.
They communicate with a variety of vocalizations.
Binturongs play a key role in seed dispersal.
They have scent glands that produce a smell like buttered popcorn.
