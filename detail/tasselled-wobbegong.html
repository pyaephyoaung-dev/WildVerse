Tasselled Wobbegong

Eucrossorhinus dasypogon

This carpet shark is distinguished by its ornate, tassel-like skin flaps that help it camouflage on the seafloor among reefs and rocky substrates.

Scientific NameEucrossorhinus dasypogon
Class / OrderChondrichthyes, Orectolobiformes
HabitatShallow coral and rocky reefs
DietCarnivore — fish and invertebrates
WeightUp to 14 kg
SizeUp to 1.3 m length
SpeedSlow-moving
LifespanUp to 25 years
RangeWestern Pacific Ocean
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown
