Quillback Rockfish

Sebastes maliger

Quillback rockfish are long-lived reef fish with distinctive quill-like spines on their dorsal fin and vibrant orange and red coloration, inhabiting rocky reefs.

Scientific NameSebastes maliger
Class / OrderActinopterygii, Scorpaeniformes
HabitatRocky reefs and kelp forests
DietCarnivore — small fish and invertebrates
WeightUp to 5 kg
SizeUp to 60 cm length
SpeedModerate swimmer
LifespanUp to 95 years
RangeNortheastern Pacific Ocean
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown
