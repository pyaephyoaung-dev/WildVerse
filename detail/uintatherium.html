Uintatherium

Uintatherium anceps

Uintatherium was a large herbivorous mammal from the Eocene known for its distinctive skull with horn-like protrusions. It was among the earliest large mammals after dinosaur extinction.

Scientific NameUintatherium anceps
Class / OrderMammalia, Dinocerata
HabitatFloodplains and forests
