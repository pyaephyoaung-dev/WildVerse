Yangtze Giant Softshell Turtle

Rafetus swinhoei

The Yangtze giant softshell turtle is one of the rarest turtles in the world, possibly the largest freshwater turtle. Critically endangered with only a few known individuals alive.

Scientific NameRafetus swinhoei
Class / OrderReptilia, Testudines
HabitatFreshwater lakes and rivers
DietOmnivore — fish, plants, carrion
Weight70-100 kg
SizeUp to 1 m carapace length
SpeedSlow-moving aquatic
Lifespan80-100 years
RangeYangtze River basin, China and Vietnam
Conservation StatusCR — Critically Endangered
PopulationCritically endangered; possibly only 3 known individuals
