Yellow Tang

Zebrasoma flavescens

The yellow tang is a vibrant, bright yellow reef fish popular in marine aquariums, known for its herbivorous diet and importance in coral reef health.

Scientific NameZebrasoma flavescens
Class / OrderActinopterygii, Perciformes
HabitatCoral reefs
DietHerbivore — algae
WeightUp to 0.3 kg
SizeUp to 20 cm length
SpeedModerate swimmer
LifespanUp to 30 years
RangePacific Ocean, especially Hawaii
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationCommon in wild
