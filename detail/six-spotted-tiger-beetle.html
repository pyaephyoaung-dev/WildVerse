Six-spotted Tiger Beetle

Cicindela sexguttata

This fast-running beetle has metallic green coloration with six white spots on its elytra. It preys on small arthropods on woodland paths and sandy areas.

Scientific NameCicindela sexguttata
Class / OrderInsecta, Coleoptera
HabitatWoodland trails and riverbanks
DietCarnivore — small insects
Weight50-70 mg
Size1.2–1.5 cm length
SpeedFast runner, up to 9 km/h
Lifespan1-2 years
RangeNorth America
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown
