Carolina Anole

Anolis carolinensis

The Carolina Anole is a small lizard native to the southeastern United States. It is known for its adaptive color changes and territorial displays involving a colorful dewlap.

Interesting Facts

It can change color from green to brown for camouflage.
Males display a pink or red dewlap during courtship.
They primarily eat insects and other small invertebrates.
Carolina Anoles can regenerate lost tails.
