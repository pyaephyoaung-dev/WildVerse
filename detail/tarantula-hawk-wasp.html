Tarantula Hawk Wasp

Pepsis grossa

This large wasp is famous for hunting tarantulas to provision its larvae. Its sting is one of the most painful insect stings known to science.

Scientific NamePepsis grossa
Class / OrderInsecta, Hymenoptera
HabitatDeserts and open woodlands
DietAdults feed on nectar; larvae feed on tarantulas
Weight1-2 g
Size5–7.5 cm length
SpeedFast flyer
Lifespan2-3 months
RangeSouthwestern United States and Mexico
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown

Interesting Facts

Females sting and paralyze tarantulas.
Males are harmless and feed on nectar.
Can fly long distances in search of prey.
Bright blue-black body with orange wings.
