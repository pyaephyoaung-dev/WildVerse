Velvet Ant

Mutillidae family

Velvet ants are actually wasps with hairy, brightly colored bodies. Females are wingless and known for their extremely painful sting.

Scientific NameMutillidae family
Class / OrderInsecta, Hymenoptera
HabitatDry, sandy areas and grasslands
DietLarvae are parasitoids; adults feed on nectar
Weight20-60 mg
Size1–2 cm length
SpeedFast runner
Lifespan1 year
RangeWorldwide
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown

Interesting Facts

Females produce a loud squeaking sound when threatened.
Larvae are parasitoids of other insects.
Bright colors serve as warning to predators.
Commonly mistaken for ants due to appearance.
