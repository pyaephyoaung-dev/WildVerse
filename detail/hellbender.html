Hellbender

Cryptobranchus alleganiensis

The Hellbender is a large aquatic salamander native to North America. It is notable for its flattened body and loose skin folds that aid in respiration.

Scientific NameCryptobranchus alleganiensis
Class / OrderAmphibia, Caudata
HabitatFast-flowing streams and rivers
DietCarnivore — crayfish, small fish
Weight0.5-1.4 kg
Size30–74 cm total length
SpeedSlow-moving
Lifespan25-30 years
RangeEastern United States
Conservation StatusNT — Near Threatened
PopulationDeclining
