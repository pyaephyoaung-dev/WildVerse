Common Midwife Toad

Alytes obstetricans

Named for the male's unique behavior of carrying fertilized eggs wrapped around his hind legs. This small toad is nocturnal and terrestrial.

Scientific NameAlytes obstetricans
Class / OrderAmphibia, Anura
HabitatWoodlands and grasslands
DietCarnivore — insects and invertebrates
Weight10-30 g
Size4–5 cm snout-vent length
SpeedModerate
Lifespan8-15 years
RangeWestern Europe
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationStable

Interesting Facts

Males take care of eggs until they hatch.
Can produce a distinctive call to attract mates.
Egg carrying reduces predation risks.
Feeds on insects and small invertebrates.
