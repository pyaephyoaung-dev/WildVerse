Eurasian Lynx

Lynx lynx

The Eurasian lynx is a medium-sized wild cat with tufted ears and a short tail. It is a solitary and elusive predator, preying on deer and smaller mammals. This species is important for maintaining balanced forest ecosystems.

Scientific NameLynx lynx
Class / OrderMammalia, Carnivora
HabitatTemperate and boreal forests
DietCarnivore — small to medium mammals and birds
Weight18-30 kg
Size80–130 cm body length
Speed50 km/h
Lifespan12-17 years
RangeEurope and Asia
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
Population~10,000 in the wild

Interesting Facts

Eurasian lynx have excellent night vision.
They use stealth and ambush tactics to hunt.
Their ear tufts improve hearing.
They are solitary except during mating season.
