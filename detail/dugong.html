Dugong

Dugong dugon

Dugongs are large marine mammals closely related to manatees, feeding primarily on seagrass. They are vulnerable due to habitat degradation and accidental capture in fishing nets.

Scientific NameDugong dugon
Class / OrderMammalia, Sirenia
HabitatCoastal shallow waters and seagrass beds
DietHerbivore — seagrass
Weight230-500 kg
Size2.4–4 m length
SpeedUp to 10–15 km/h
Lifespan70 years
RangeIndo-Pacific coastal waters
Conservation StatusVU — Vulnerable
PopulationVulnerable; estimated ~7,000–15,000
