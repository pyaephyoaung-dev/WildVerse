Vampire Squid

Vampyroteuthis infernalis

The vampire squid is a small deep-sea cephalopod with dark coloration and webbing between its arms, adapted to oxygen-poor environments with unique feeding strategies.

Scientific NameVampyroteuthis infernalis
Class / OrderCephalopoda, Vampyromorphida
HabitatOxygen minimum zones in deep ocean
DietDetritivore — marine snow and small particles
WeightUp to 1 kg
SizeUp to 30 cm total length
SpeedSlow-moving
LifespanUp to 4 years
RangeWorldwide tropical and temperate oceans
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown

Interesting Facts

Feeds on marine detritus, not blood.
Has light-producing photophores.
Can invert webbing to appear larger.
Name inspired by dark color and cloak-like webbing.
