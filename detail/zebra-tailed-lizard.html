Zebra-tailed Lizard

Callisaurus draconoides

The Zebra-tailed Lizard is a fast-moving desert dweller famous for its black and white striped tail. It uses its tail to distract predators while it escapes.

Scientific NameCallisaurus draconoides
Class / OrderReptilia, Squamata
HabitatDesert and arid regions
DietInsectivore — insects
Weight15-20 g
Size10–15 cm total length
SpeedFast runner
Lifespan3-5 years
RangeSouthwestern United States and northwestern Mexico
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern
PopulationUnknown

Interesting Facts

It runs on its hind legs at high speeds.
The striped tail is waved to confuse predators.
It primarily eats insects.
It shelters in burrows during the heat of day.
