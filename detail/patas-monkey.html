Patas Monkey

Erythrocebus patas

The patas monkey is a fast-running primate adapted to savanna and open woodland habitats. It is known for its reddish coat and ground-dwelling behavior. Patas monkeys live in social groups and feed on insects, seeds, and fruits.

Scientific NameErythrocebus patas
Class / OrderMammalia, Primates
HabitatSavannas and open woodlands
DietOmnivore — fruits, insects, small animals
Weight5-7 kg
Size40–70 cm body length
Speed55 km/h
Lifespan15-20 years
RangeWest and East Africa
Conservation StatusLC — Least Concern

Interesting Facts

Patas monkeys can run at speeds up to 55 km/h.
They have cheek pouches for food storage.
They live in multi-male, multi-female groups.
Males have bright blue scrotal skin.
