Uakari

Cacajao calvus

The uakari is a striking primate with a bright red face and short tail, adapted to flooded Amazonian forests. It feeds on seeds, fruits, and insects. Uakaris are social animals living in groups and are known for their strong jaws.

Interesting Facts

Uakaris have extremely strong jaws to crack hard seeds.
Their red face helps signal health to mates.
They have a short tail compared to other monkeys.
Uakaris are excellent swimmers.
